Introduction

Ownership structure defines who owns and controls a business.

Sole Proprietorship

  • Owned by one person
  • Easy to start
  • Full control

Joint Hindu Family Business

  • Owned by family
  • Managed by Karta
  • Traditional system

Partnership

  • Two or more owners
  • Shared profits
  • Agreement required

Co-operative Society

  • Owned by members
  • Equal rights
  • Service motive

Joint Stock Company

  • Owned by shareholders
  • Separate legal entity
  • Large scale business

Comparison

  • Sole → Single owner
  • Partnership → Shared
  • Company → Shareholders

Conclusion

Different ownership structures suit different business needs.
